### Next, we had our ***licensed*** !![TheatreSports™](https://improvlore.com/event/the-great-face-off/)!!
@ Sat, 27th June

(photos to be added)

If you've never watched Lagaan or Gangs of Wasseypur before, it's a good thing you skipped this show - the spoilers were brutal! 
This weekend we had teams take on each other to win points and the love of the audience, by coming out on top in a series of tireless challenges. From reality TV to talking degrees, from emotional road trips to diss tracks about the opponents - we made them all up on the spot!


### Last up we finished !![Improv 101](https://improvlore.com/101)!!
@ Sat, 27th June & Sun, 28th June

![PHOTO-2026-06-28-15-54-37 =half](https://img.improvlore.com/uploads/2026-06-28/b12d8f46-1c31-458f-b2ee-df466553cc04.jpg)

How does one not panic on stage? I mean, really? There are lights, an audience full of strangers, palms are sweaty, knees weak, arms are heavy... You get the gist. Our recently concluded 12-hour course might not eliminate panic, but it certainly gets one to a good place where they may learn to manage it. We saw some excellent scenework, shared some deep insights, and came out feeling confident, creative, and comfortable in a new community of friends. 

come join us next week as we start July with a busy busy week!
